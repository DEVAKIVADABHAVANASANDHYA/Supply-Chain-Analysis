# Supply Chain Operations Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing supplier performance and supply chain operations using Power BI dashboards.

The objective of the analysis is to identify operational inefficiencies, transportation delays, supplier risks, and quality issues to support better business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze supplier performance
- Identify high-risk suppliers
- Reduce delivery delays
- Improve operational efficiency
- Monitor defect rates and logistics performance

---

## 📈 Key KPIs
- Profit
- Profit Margin
- Average Shipping Time
- Average Defect Rate
- Supplier Performance Score

---

## 📊 Dashboards Included
### 1. Supply Chain Operations Dashboard
- Revenue Analysis
- Shipping Analysis
- Transportation Lead Time
- Operational KPIs

### 2. Supplier Performance Dashboard
- Supplier Risk Matrix
- Lead Time vs Defect Analysis
- High-Risk Supplier Identification
- Performance Insights

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Supplier 3 drives the highest delivery delays and defect risk
- Supplier 1 performs best with the lowest defect rate and fastest delivery
- Air transport contributes to the highest lead time
- Most suppliers operate in the moderate performance zone

---

## ✅ Recommendations
- Prioritize high-performing suppliers
- Reduce dependency on high-risk suppliers
- Optimize transportation strategies
- Continuously monitor supplier performance
